What is a Graduate Apprenticeship?

A Graduate Apprenticehsip is a method of study in which a student is in full-time employment and attends University on day-release. The student doesn't undertake as many modules as their full-time counterparts, instead getting some of their Unversity credits from evidencing work-based learning.
